How To Reach Us
===============

* * *

  

  

* * *

  
Chandigarh, the "City Beautiful", is located at the foot-hills of Shivaliks. The CCET (Degree Wing) campus is midway between the Chandigarh Railway station and ISBT. It is approximately 4 km from the Chandigarh Railway station and ISBT on Madhya Marg in Sector-26, Chandigarh.
